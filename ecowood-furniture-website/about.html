About EcoWood Furniture

Our journey to sustainable furniture production

Our Story

Founded in 2018 by a group of environmentally conscious craftsmen, EcoWood Furniture was born out of a passion for sustainable living and quality craftsmanship.

We started as a small workshop in East Java with just three artisans creating bespoke furniture pieces from reclaimed wood. Today, we've grown into a team of 25 dedicated individuals who share our vision of creating beautiful, functional furniture while minimizing environmental impact.

Each piece of furniture is handcrafted with attention to detail, ensuring that our customers receive not just furniture, but heirlooms that can be cherished for generations.

Our Mission

At EcoWood Furniture, our mission is to create sustainable, high-quality furniture that brings both beauty and functionality to homes while minimizing our environmental footprint.

We believe that furniture production doesn't have to come at the expense of our planet. By using sustainable and reclaimed materials, implementing eco-friendly production processes, and emphasizing craftsmanship, we aim to set a new standard in the furniture industry.

We're committed to supporting local communities by sourcing materials from responsible suppliers and providing fair employment opportunities.
